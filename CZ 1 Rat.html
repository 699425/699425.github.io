The Rat

subtitle

What is it?

The Rat is the first sign of the Chinese zodiac, representing people born in the Year of the Rat.

Background Story

According to Chinese mythology, the Jade Emperor held a race to determine the order of the zodiac animals. The Rat, being clever, hitched a ride on the back of the Ox and jumped off just before the finish line to claim first place.

The day before the “Great Race,” the rat and the cat made a promise that the rat will wake the cat up to join the race. But on the day of the race, the rat and the cat both were not in the race. The animals raced, but when the ox was about to become the first to finish the race, the rat jumped off of the ox's head and became the first to finish the race. When the cat woke up the race was already over, so afterwards the cat started to chase the rat.

Personality Traits

People born in the Year of the Rat are often described as quick-thinking, resourceful, and charming. They are known for their intelligence and ability to adapt to new situations.
