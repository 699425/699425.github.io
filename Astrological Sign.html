The Astrological Signs

What is it?

Astrological signs are twelve 30-degree sectors of the zodiac (Aries to Pisces) that correspond to the sun's position at birth, representing specific personality traits, elements (Fire, Earth, Air, Water), and modalities. Based on the Babylonian zodiac, these signs determine horoscopes, with key dates generally spanning from March 21 (Aries) to March 20 (Pisces).

History

Created by the Babylonians around 500 BCE, it was named "circle of animals" (zōdiakos kyklos) by the Greeks because most signs represent animals.

The Western zodiac system was developed by the Greeks and later refined by the Romans. It is based on the division of the ecliptic (the path of the sun across the sky) into 12 equal segments, each associated with a specific sign.
